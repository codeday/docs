# Sponsors

- Sponsorship information is also available online at [https://www.srnd.org/sponsor](https://www.srnd.org/sponsor)
- You can customize the amounts for your city, just make sure to be consistent.
- If you want to customize what you offer, please check in with SRND HQ first — there are some things we cannot offer (e.g. lists of attendee emails).

- Once you have a sponsor, you can send them over to subsections of [https://www.srnd.org/sponsor/guide](https://www.srnd.org/sponsor/guide)
