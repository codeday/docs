# Registering on Showcase

CodeDay will present awards for the best project in several categories, across all projects submitted in the world:

![AwardSticker_World Champion.png](Registering%20on%20Showcase/AwardSticker_World_Champion.png)

- World Champion, Original Music
- World Champion, Original Art
- World Champion, Creative Concept

To be eligible, your attendees need to create a project on [Showcase.CodeDay.org](http://Showcase.CodeDay.org) with:

- All team members added (or we won’t be able to give them their awards)
- At least one screenshot **or screen recording if they have music, voice acting, etc.**
- Brief description.

<aside>
⚠️ You’ll need to start on this step as early as 7-8am to get it done in time.

</aside>

(Projects on Showcase will also show up on your region’s CodeDay page, on CodeDay.org, and in our Twitch livestreams!)
