# CodeDay Venue Information

# CodeDay Provides:

- Event staffing
- Food, drink, and snacks
- $2M/occur liability insurance
- Waivers (we can also have students sign yours)

# Requirements

## Venue Limitations

- Must allow high school students, preferably also college and some middle school students. (We will provide appropriate ratios of chaperones.)
- Most events are 24-hours (overnight). Individual event teams may be ok with a 2-day or 1-day event. Check with your event team for more.
- No required catering. (Or the catering is offered at a reasonable price or donated. Our budget for lunch + dinner + snacks is $12/student.)

## Layout

- Accommodates the expected number of students (most CodeDay events are at least 50 students, and many cities are as large as 400):
    - Students spend most of the event seated at tables in either a classroom format, or around banquet tables or rounds, at least 3 to a table.
    - We prefer a few big rooms to many small rooms.
- If the space has off-limits areas, it should be possible to mark them with a sign, or block them off, so students are not able to access.
- Only one main entrance (or ability to block off alternate entrances)
- Access to bathrooms.
- **Nice-To-Haves:**
    - Dedicated kickoff area (theater style seating or standing room)
    - Dedicated workshop area (conference room or an area of the venue with support for 10-20 students)
    - Access to a kitchen.
    - Free or cheap parking.

## A/V & Amenities

- Tables (banquet tables or rounds) + chairs for expected attendance. We do not require table cloths.
- Power drops at each table.
- Wireless internet access.
    - Please ensure the following pages are not blocked: [codeday.to](http://codeday.to), [codeday.org](http://www.codeday.org), [showcase.codeday.org](http://showcase.codeday.org), [playcodecup.com](http://playcodecup.com), and [editor.construct.net](http://editor.construct.net)
- Access to projector and voice amplification system of some sort. Or, if not, an area where we can set up a rented projector.
- **Nice-To-Haves:**
    - Access to whiteboards
