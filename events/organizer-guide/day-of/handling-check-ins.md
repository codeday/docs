# Handling Check-ins

{: .important }
**All participants must be between the ages of 12-25, and:**
- Participants who are 20 or older must be a current student.
- Participants who are under 14 may not stay overnight.

If you're assigned to check-in, you can open [clear.codeday.org](https://clear.codeday.org/dayof), click on your event, and then click the "eye" next to Tickets to process checkins.

![Untitled](../Handling%20Check-ins/Untitled.png)

Once you're on the checkin page, you'll see a list of registrations, with a checkin/checkout button:

![Untitled](../Handling%20Check-ins/Untitled%201.png)

## Some participants might be missing waivers

{: .important }
Everyone needs a waiver, including event staff. The only exceptions are judges, family members who are watching kickoff/presentations, and venue employees.

![Untitled](../Handling%20Check-ins/Untitled%202.png)

Participants missing a waiver must fill one out before they can check in:

- **If they are a minor:** have their parent go to codeday.to/Waiver
- **If they are not a minor:** have them go to codeday.to/AdultWaiver

Once the waiver is filled out, ask them to show you the confirmation screen (or confirmation email).

## All students and mentors get a nametag

![Nametag.png](../Handling%20Check-ins/Nametag.png)

Every participant needs to make and wear a nametag, because it helps us identify who is allowed to be at the event. Parents and other community members won't have a nametag, and can be asked to leave after kickoff.

## Everyone who can leave gets a wristband

{: .important }
***EVERYONE*** who wants to leave ***MUST*** have a wristband, even staff. This makes it easy to monitor the doors.

![Wristband.png](../Handling%20Check-ins/Wristband.png)

Give a "you can leave if you want" wristband to everyone who can leave:

- Event staff.
- Participants who are older than your local age of majority (usually 18, but some places are 17 or 19).
- Anyone who brings in a letter from their parent saying they are allowed to leave. (Please save the letter until the end of the event.)
- Anyone whose parent tells you during check-in that they are allowed to leave.
