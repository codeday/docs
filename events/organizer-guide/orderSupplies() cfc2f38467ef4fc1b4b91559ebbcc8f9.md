# orderSupplies()

**You must [fill out this form](https://www.codeday.org/f/codeday-supplies) no later than two weeks before your event (one month for international events).**

You will receive the following standard event supplies:

- Name tags, pens, and wristbands for check-in
- Awards, award stickers, and [judging info-sheets](Before%20Judging%20bd7617f44f0c49958ce34a82e63c77bd.md)
- [Signs](../Posters%20and%20PDFs/Sign%20b25781359f254704b6d7c09a537f30cf.md)
- Stickers to give to attendees
- ID badges

## Sponsor stretch goal supplies

If you raised more than your event’s sponsorship minimum, we will also send you:

- $100 above minimum: Trophies
- $350 above minimum: CodeDay volunteer shirts
- $10 * [expected yearly students]: Everyone gets a CodeDay t-shirt
