# Photos and Videos

If you have a photographer or a videographer, here’s a suggested list of shots to get:

- Event Collateral B-Roll
    - Event signs
    - Swag
    - Anything with sponsor logos
- Crowd B-Roll
    - Venue establishing shots, showing scale of event
    - Students working in groups
    - Students in workshops
    - Students doing fun activities
    - Shot from stage at kickoff and closing
- Projects B-Roll
    - Presentations
    - Student computers close-up when working
    - Student typing on keyboards close-up
- Speakers
    - Organizers at kickoff
    - Workshop presenters
    - End presentations
    - Any sponsors speaking
- Award Presentations
- Soundbites
    - Students
        - “describe your project for us”
        - “did you have a plan going into codeday?”
        - “how did you get people together for a team?”
        - “what inspired you to come to CodeDay?”
        - “what have you learned during codeday?”
        - “what do you like about coding?”
        - “favorite thing about codeday?”
        - “how was your first codeday experience?”
        - (artists/musicians) “how do art and code go hand in hand?”
    - Volunteers
        - “Why do you volunteer?”
        - “How does helping students make you feel?”
        - “What do you do as a volunteer?”
        - “What’s your favorite thing about CodeDay?”
