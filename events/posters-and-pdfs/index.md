## CodeDay Mini Promotion Materials

### Student Focused

{: .note }
> **CodeDay: The most beginner friendly event for building games and apps!**
> CodeDay is a worldwide event where artists, programmers, musicians, actors, and writers get together to build apps and games. You can make anything you want at CodeDay, the only requirement is to be creative.
> There are absolutely NO requirements to participate, there will be workshops on game development tools and mentors to help you any time you get stuck.
>
> We can't wait to see what awesome project you are going to create! Register now at ____

### Teacher/parent focused

{: .note }
> **CodeDay: Student programmers and artists work together to make something awesome**
>
> At CodeDay, students pitch ideas for apps or games, then work in teams to make their ideas a reality with code. At the end, students present to judges.
> Most students register with little-to-no interest in Computer Science, but 70% of these students continue coding for months after the event, 65% register for a CS class, and 48% pursue a CS major in college.
> CodeDay events are run by our staff who complete our extensive Code Evangelist training: the most in-depth training for helping students succeed workshops and mentoring.

### Example projects from previous events

**Time Cube** - a platformer game where you need to use the powers of time travel to win. View this project at [https://codeday.sh/zh](https://codeday.sh/zh)

**Thorns: A Lost Conversation** - a visual novel game with a twist: words hurt. See more at [https://codeday.sh/thorns](https://codeday.sh/thorns)

**The MonkeyBots** - a DIY battle bot made out of unconventional parts and tools. Watch it demolish a pile of soda cans at [https://codeday.sh/monkeybot](https://codeday.sh/monkeybot)
