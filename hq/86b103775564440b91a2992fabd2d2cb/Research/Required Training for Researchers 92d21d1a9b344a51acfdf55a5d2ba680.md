# Required Training for Researchers

<aside>
📌 If you have already received training on responsible research and conducting research with human subjects (such as when you were studying at a university), do not follow this page’s instructions.

Email your completion certificate/transcript/other proof to: research@codeday.org

</aside>

Staff involved in conducting research funded by the U.S. Federal Government are required to receive training in research ethics. CodeDay provides this training through CITI Program.

You will need to pay for these courses using a Divvy card from a program budget. If you do not have access to Divvy or the appropriate budget, ask your supervisor or CodeDay contact to add you.

1. Go to [https://about.citiprogram.org/](https://about.citiprogram.org/) and register for an account as an “Independent Learner”.
2. Register for and complete **“Responsible Conduct of Research (RCR) Basic”**. Don't register for continuing education credits.
3. Register for and complete **“Human Subjects Research — Social-Behavioral-Educational (SBE) Comprehensive”**. Don't register for continuing education credits.
4. Submit your completion certificate and transcript for both courses to research@codeday.org. If you have a CodeDay account, please include your username from [https://account.codeday.org/](https://account.codeday.org/) 

<aside>
📌 CITI program provides two options for how to take courses: interactive or traditional. Make sure to try both, but note that interactive is significantly slower for most people.

</aside>
