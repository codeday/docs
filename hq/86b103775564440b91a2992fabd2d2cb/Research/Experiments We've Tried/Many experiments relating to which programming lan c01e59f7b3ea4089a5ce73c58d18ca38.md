# Many experiments relating to which programming language and/or framework is best, including: Construct 2/3, Scratch, Python, Pygame, PICO-8, Unity, Gamemaker, Lua-based game mods, HTML/CSS/Javascript, Javascript

Result: Construct 2/3 was the best for completion and 2.5mo outcomes for total beginners, at 9%-points better outcomes than Scratch in completion and 2.5mo (but slightly worse than Scratch for students with some prior coding experience). Gamemaker was worse than Construct or Scratch for total beginners and those with some experience.

HTML/CSS/JS had the interesting property of being comparable to Scratch for completion, but near the bottom of the list at 2.5mo follow-up.

All other coding languages we tried were dramatically worse than Construct or Scratch on all metrics by 10-20%-points. PICO-8 was the worst with only 30% of students completing and no one continuing at 2.5mo followup.
