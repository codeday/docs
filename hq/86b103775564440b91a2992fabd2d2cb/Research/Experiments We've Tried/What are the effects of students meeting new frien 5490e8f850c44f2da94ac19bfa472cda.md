# What are the effects of students meeting new friends at an event on their chances of completing an event, and 2.5mo outcomes? Does it vary by number of friends, experience level of the student, and/or experience level of the friend?

Result: Students who said they made a friend at CodeDay about 15% more likely to complete the event vs those who did not. There was a similar effect on long-term outcomes, but this is probably better attributed to the fact that the student completed the event. The number of friends was not important.

New learners who made friends who were more experienced were about 10% more likely to report continuing to code 2.5mo later, vs those who made friends who were also new learners.
