# What is the effect of perceived and actual ticket cost on the number of students who register, and the number of students who attend an event? (Broken down by experience level.)

Result: $0 advertised tickets had overall between 40-60% no-show rates depending on the event. The no-shows were unevenly distributed by experience: total beginner no-show rate was between 70-80% while advanced attendees only made up 10% of no-shows.

There is no noticeable difference between when we actually charged $10, vs when we said the tickets were $10 but gave promotion codes to every school.
