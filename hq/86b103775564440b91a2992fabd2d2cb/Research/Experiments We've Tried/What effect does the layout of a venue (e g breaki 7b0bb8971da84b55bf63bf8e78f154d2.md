# What effect does the layout of a venue (e.g. breaking things into conference rooms) have on the chances of a student to make new friends at an event? What is the ideal number of students to have in a room?

Result: CodeDay has been held in many different venues with rooms of different sizes.

The number of students who reported making a friend increases as the number of people in one room increases, up until about 100 people in a room. Having 200+ people in one room is about as bad as having all teams in their own break-out rooms.

The best configuration - 100 people per large room - had 12% more students reporting having made a friend over the worst configuration - team-specific break-out rooms or very large rooms.
