# What are the effects of having a conversation with students during check-in on the willingness of that student to ask for help from mentors, and the chance of that student meeting new friends at the event? What is the optimal length of the conversation?

Result: At some events where staff greeted students at the door, staff members reported more interactions with students (18% more of the event's participants), and more students reported meeting a friend (5% more vs events of similar size).

Some events did not show this effect, which may suggest it was not actually done or not done in a way that made students more comfortable. Overall we think this is worth encouraging but it's difficult to control.
