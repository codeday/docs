# Which experiences induce students to share social media photos with their peers, and what is the effect of sharing photos on the number of students interested in attending future CodeDays?

Result: Interesting, noteworthy events (such as the no-hands programming contest) were more effective than staged photo-ops or branded Snapchat filters. Events with noteworthy events had 5-point larger share of word-of-mouth registrations in subsequent events. No difference for staged photo-ops.
