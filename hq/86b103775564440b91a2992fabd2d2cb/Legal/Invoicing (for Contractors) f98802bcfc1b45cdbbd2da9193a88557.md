# Invoicing (for Contractors)

In order to pay you, you will need to email an invoice to accounts@codeday.org. Invoices must include the following:

- Your name.
- The date.
- A unique (to you) invoice number. You can make this up, just don’t reuse the same number.
- A list of what you are billing us for, including a description, quantity, and rate:
    - For most people, you can put “hourly consulting, <time period>”
    - If you are working with mentors at CodeDay Labs, this is probably “Mentor Managed”, “Mentor Recruited”, etc.
- Total amount

For simplicity, you can use the following template: [https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1KxmELS16OkhdQ_d1YwHpd3sLpbjL6QBX67NVja2quF4/edit?usp=sharing](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1KxmELS16OkhdQ_d1YwHpd3sLpbjL6QBX67NVja2quF4/edit?usp=sharing)
