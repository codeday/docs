# Payroll Approval Process

Everyone must ensure their hours are correct in Toggl every week. You are encouraged to use the Toggl desktop app to log hours.

When you are logging hours, fill out the information as follows:

- **“What have you done?” (text description):** not required unless specified by your supervisor.
- **Project (folder icon):**
    - If your project is part of a government grant: please select the name of the government grant.
    - If your project is NOT part of a government grant: select the appropriate program under “No Client”.
- **Tags:** not required unless specified by your supervisor.
- **Mark as Billable:** do not check.

If you have any hours billed to a government grant, your supervisor will review your time once a month and provide you with a copy of a certification form for you to fill out and sign.
