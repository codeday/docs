# Updating Payouts Information

## Logging into Payouts Dashboard

Visit [https://account.codeday.org/](https://account.codeday.org/) and sign in *using the same account you used to apply for Labs*.

Once you’re logged in, scroll down until you see “Open Payouts Dashboard (new window)”:

![Untitled](Updating%20Payouts%20Information/Untitled.png)

Once you click the button, the site will open a pop-up with our banking provider, Stripe. You may need to disable your pop-up blocker.

![Untitled](Updating%20Payouts%20Information/Untitled%201.png)

In this new window, click on the User icon at the top-right to be redirected to the “Your account” page.

![Untitled](Updating%20Payouts%20Information/Untitled%202.png)

## Updating Bank Account

In the “Your account” page, scroll down until you see the “Payout details” section.

![Untitled](Updating%20Payouts%20Information/Untitled%203.png)

Click the edit button next to your your Payout account (NOT your “Linked external account”). You’ll see a sidebar open:

![Untitled](Updating%20Payouts%20Information/Untitled%204.png)

Choose the “link another account” hyperlink, above the “Save” button:

![Untitled](Updating%20Payouts%20Information/bc8149c4-1064-476f-9b02-798568803b52.png)

You’ll be prompted for your online banking details in order to confirm your account immediately. (You can click “enter bank account details manually” if you would prefer not to provide this information, but you may be asked to verify with test deposits which can take several days.)

![Untitled](Updating%20Payouts%20Information/Untitled%205.png)

![Untitled](Updating%20Payouts%20Information/Untitled%206.png)

![Untitled](Updating%20Payouts%20Information/Untitled%207.png)

Once your new account has been linked, select your new preferred account and click “Save”:

![Untitled](Updating%20Payouts%20Information/a411b9d8-e15d-401d-920a-dd0c816d020c.png)
