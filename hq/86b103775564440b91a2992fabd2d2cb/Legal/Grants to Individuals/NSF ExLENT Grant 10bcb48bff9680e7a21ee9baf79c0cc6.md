# NSF ExLENT Grant

# Charitable Purpose

The purpose of this fellowship grant is to allow college students to pursue their education with a focus on AI-related careers.

# Selection Criteria

## Eligibility

Students attending Community and Technical Colleges who are pursuing a degree in Computer Science or related fields. The school must have agreed to partner with CodeDay and MinT previously. Students must be US citizens or permanent residents.

## Evaluation

Spots will be allocated among partner schools roughly in proportion to how many students successfully completed CodeDay and MinT programs in the last year. Specific students will be selected by faulty on the basis of how likely they are to complete the program, interest in AI careers, and the program’s likely impact on that student’s career relative to other students.

## Staff

Faculty from partner schools will be responsible for determining which students can enroll. CodeDay staff will not have input into eligibility.

Individuals involved in the selection are subject to CodeDay’s Conflict of Interest Policy. They may not have a direct or indirect interest in the selection of particular recipients.

## Renewals

The grant does not allow renewal proposals outside of the ordinary application process.

Any applicants who have previously received individual grants, whether considered renewals or not, will be screened to verify they have submitted all required reports at the time the grant is made, and we have no information that prior grant funding was spent in a manner other than intended.  

# Recipient Reporting

Recipients must complete 2x monthly reflections from their MinT mentor meetings, and weekly reflections during 1-2x monthly micro-internships. Payments will only be disbursed (roughly quarterly) to students who are current on these requirements.
