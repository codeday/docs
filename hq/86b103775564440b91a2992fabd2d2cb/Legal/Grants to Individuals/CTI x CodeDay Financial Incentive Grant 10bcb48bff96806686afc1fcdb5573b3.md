# CTI x CodeDay Financial Incentive Grant

# Charitable Purpose

The purpose of this grant is to help college students pursue their education in Computer Science.

# Selection Criteria

## Eligibility

Any students participating in CTI’s programs who are eligible for CodeDay Labs Summer Open-Source Experience. Specifically, this comprises students in the US (primarily California) seeking a degree in a computer-related field from a college who have met certain academic milestones set out by CTI. The majority of students come from lower-income and other under-represented backgrounds, but this is not a specific requirement of the grant.

## Evaluation

All eligible applicants will receive the grant, first-come-first-served, until grant funds are exhausted. Grant amounts will depend on the amount of hours students spend working.

## Staff

Our partner program CTI, a program of Cal State University Monterey Bay, will determine who receives the grant.

Individuals involved in the selection are subject to CodeDay’s Conflict of Interest Policy. They may not have a direct or indirect interest in the selection of particular recipients.

## Renewals

The grant does not allow renewal proposals outside of the ordinary application process.

Any applicants who have previously received individual grants, whether considered renewals or not, will be screened to verify they have submitted all required reports at the time the grant is made, and we have no information that prior grant funding was spent in a manner other than intended.  

# Recipient Reporting

Participants will need to be current on their progress through the CTI education models, and submit a monthly report, in order to receive a payment.
