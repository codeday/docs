# Email Templates

Template for contacts who are interested:

Cold email template:

> Hey [NAME_OF_MAINTAINER],
> 
> 
> 
> My name is [YOUR_NAME] and I’m reaching out to you on behalf of [CodeDay Labs](https://labs.codeday.org), a coding program for college students. I noticed [NAME_OF_PROJECT]’s big open-source impact, and I believe that our program that can greatly benefit [NAME_OF_PROJECT]. 
> 
> CodeDay Labs is a program is for college students who don't really have a grasp of 
> real-world software engineering. Often, colleges leave students with only knowledge of the theory part of computer science, not how to actually contribute to projects in the developer world.
> 
> This is what we're solving at CodeDay Labs. We're providing students with the opportunity to work on real-life projects and contribute code to something bigger than a class project. Instead of giving students dull internships doing busy work at a giant tech corporation, we're directly impacting the open source community by giving real issues on OSS projects for them to work on.
> 
> You can help introduce new opportunities to these students and benefit your OSS project at the same time **by delegating certain low-priority issues** (perhaps in your backlog of tasks) to our students. This will help them gain a foundation for real software engineering skills and greatly increase the number of contributors and stars towards your project. This is a very low commitment opportunity (hardly 1 hour of your time to set up these issues), and we have seen great results from the CodeDay Labs program. Out of our around 300 students, 75% of them kept contributing to the same OSS projects that they worked on during the program. This can vastly increase the number of contributors to [PROJECT_NAME] and the number of issues being solved. 
> 
> If this sounds like something that you are interested in, let me know so I can give you further steps. We acknowledge that you might have some questions, so feel free to shoot me an email or, if you prefer, [schedule a meeting](https://calendly.com/toukirkhan-codeday) with us.
> 
> We hope that you will consider contributing to the CodeDay Labs program. By helping out, you will truly be impacting the lives of hundreds of students by introducing them to the realm of software engineering. 
>
