# MySQL/Postgres/MSSQL

Complete the MySQL tutorial here — [https://www.mysqltutorial.org/basic-mysql-tutorial.aspx](https://www.mysqltutorial.org/basic-mysql-tutorial.aspx) — through the end of Section 7. Subqueries.

For each section, take a screenshot of the queries and results, and upload them as a gallery to [Imgur.com](http://imgur.com), then submit a link to that gallery to complete the assignment.
