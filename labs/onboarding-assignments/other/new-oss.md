## Create Your Kickoff Prep Document

Create your own editable copy of the following document: 

https://docs.google.com/document/d/1z4QpivksS8NNEhAaPNfdl-Xd-ZqgBe4SAT0_L162CTo/edit?usp=sharing

[https://docs.google.com/document/d/1z4QpivksS8NNEhAaPNfdl-Xd-ZqgBe4SAT0_L162CTo/edit](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1z4QpivksS8NNEhAaPNfdl-Xd-ZqgBe4SAT0_L162CTo/edit)

To create a copy:

- Click File
- Click “Make a Copy”
- Choose your own Google Drive
- Click “Share”
- Set “Anyone with the link” to View

**N*ow fill out your copy of the document with your own responses. This will be what you discuss in your first meetings.***

When you’re done, submit the link to your document as your onboarding assignment submission.
